## ✨ About Rubi Cafe
Rubi Cafe is an artisan café in **NGGO Colony, Erode, Tamil Nadu**, celebrating South India's coffee culture and traditional breakfast rituals. From classic **filter kaapi** served in brass dabara-tumbler sets to crisp **ghee masala dosa** and modern **Nilgiri cold brew**, we blend heritage flavors with contemporary craft.

**Established 2026** • **4.9★ Google Reviews** • **Open 7am–10pm daily**
